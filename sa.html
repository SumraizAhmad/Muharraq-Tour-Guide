SA'ADA

Sa'ada is one of Edamah's Company peojects. They are a bahraini real estat invesment company. They finished four projects in the Kingdom of bahrain which are Hawar, Bilaj Aljazayer, Saada, and Jumeirah.


Saada provides a puplic waterfront with shaded teraces and parking. The gross area is 13,112 m2, and it has got 34 shop units. the car parking spaces can hold up to 200 parkes with a designated area for handicaps. It also has got 36 marina bays with a lovely view of Manama. The project is based around 6 main buldings each of them has 2 floors at least.
