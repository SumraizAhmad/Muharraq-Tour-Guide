MUHARRAQ SOUQ Display

Muharraq souq is one of the largest markets in the country of Bahrain. The Souq is located at the border of the island near the sea. You can go to Muharraq souq from Shaikh Hamad Highway if you come from Manama. The Souq is located near Shaikh Isa Bin Ali Mosque. The people in the Souq are very welcoming, and lots of free food is offered on the way.


We recommend going in the early morning, especially in the winter season. There are a couple of places where you can eat, but we recommend eating at Alrayan Restaurant. Al Rayan restaurant is very well designed with different sections where you can enjoy eating indoors or outdoors. They use a boat as their kitchen , which you wouldn't expect to find there. The food is delicious, and they offer different dishes, mainly from Bahraini cuisine and some from Indian cuisine. This restaurant is the only restaurant in Muharraq where you can eat on a tree!

In Muharram souq, you can find different types of items to purchase. Muharraq souq is popular with the traditional sweet of Bahrain called Hallwa. Many brand names sell Hallwa in Muharraq souq Like alkhulood and almurooj, shawater brand is the one which dominates the making of Hallwa in the country. Other than sweets, you can find plenty of shops which sell spices, clothes, jewellery, electronics, and many other goods.


The vibes are lovely in the souq, people are friendly. its a really good place to find peace and culture.
