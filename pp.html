PEARLING PATH

pearling path is one of Muharraq finest projects, the Pearling industry is what Bahrain economy was based on back in the days. The project started in 2011 and is still going on. At the moment, the Pearling Path Visitor Center, Nukhidhah House, Bu Mahir Visitor Center, and Qal'at Bu Mahir are open to the public.


Pearling Path should conect more than 16 historical houses in Bahrain. The houses belong to Shaikhs, Tradors, and big business families.

PEARLING PATH Gallery

The Pearling Path is a great way to learn about Muharraq and how the people lived back in the day. The cultural office in Bahrin provides tour guides which is amazing where you can get details about every building and stories of each corner of Muharraq but just walking there is meditating as well.
